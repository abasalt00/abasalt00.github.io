About

Physics-trained.
Hardware-obsessed.

I hold a PhD in Physics from Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz and have worked across Germany, Japan, Brazil, and the US — tackling hard problems at the edge of what's technically possible.

I specialize in taking a concept from idea to working instrument. That means designing the PCB, writing the firmware, building the enclosure, running the experiment, and processing the data. All of it, end to end.

Currently at UCLA Health, building miniaturized electrophysiology systems for freely moving animals. Co-founder of QuSpectra, a NASA SBIR-funded startup building miniaturized quantum sensors for label-free molecular detection.
