## About Me

I'm a physics-trained engineer building hardware at the intersection of neuroscience and biomedical technology. I hold a PhD in Physics and have worked across Germany, Japan, Brazil, and the US — tackling hard problems at the edge of what's technically possible.

I specialize in taking a concept from idea to working instrument — designing the PCB, writing the firmware, building the enclosure, running the experiment, and processing the data. All of it, end to end.

Currently at **UCLA Health**, building miniaturized electrophysiology systems for freely moving animals. Co-founder of **QuSpectra**, a NASA-funded deep tech startup developing miniaturized quantum sensors for label-free molecular detection.

Drop me a message if you want to build something hard.
