## QuSpectra — Quantum Sensor (NASA SBIR Funded)

A 50g miniaturized quantum sensor capable of detecting signals from samples too small for most instruments. Built for NASA Phase I — applicable to disease detection, drug purity verification, food safety, and environmental monitoring. Co-founded the startup QuSpectra.

[GitHub →](https://github.com/abasalt00/NV-quantum-sensor)

## Miniaturized Neural Recording System

A 1.07g PCB stack that records 32 channels of neural data, tracks head movement via IMU, and monitors eye position with the NanEye camera — all while riding on a freely moving mouse. Designed every layer: PCB layout, firmware, and mechanical integration.

[GitHub →](https://github.com/abasalt00/ephys-module)
